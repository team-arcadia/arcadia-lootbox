<br>

<p align="center"><strong>Arcadia LootBox</strong> is a premium, feature-rich lootbox system designed for the <strong>Arcadia: Echoes of Power</strong> server.<br>Powered by <strong>Arcadia Lib</strong>, it offers dual lootbox types, 50 integrated keys, free timed lootboxes, Arcadia Hub integration, and complete configuration.</p>

<h3 align="center">Key Features</h3>

<p align="center">
<strong>Dual Lootbox Types</strong> — "Weighted" (each item rolls with its own %) and "Guaranteed" (one random item + guaranteed drop).<br>
<strong>50 Key Items</strong> — Dungeon, Shop, Vote, Lootable, Event, Boss keys across 10 tiers with custom textures.<br>
<strong>Free Timed Lootboxes</strong> — Configurable cooldown (72h default), reducible to 48h with permissions. Works per-lootbox.<br>
<strong>Arcadia Hub Integration</strong> — Steampunk-themed client-side lootbox browser with configurable shop link.<br>
<strong>6 Rarity Tiers</strong> — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic with colored display and broadcasts.<br>
<strong>Server Broadcasts</strong> — Announce rare drops server-wide with configurable rarity threshold.<br>
<strong>Soft LuckPerms</strong> — Full LuckPerms integration that works gracefully without LP installed.<br>
<strong>18 Admin Commands</strong> — Complete management under <code>/arcadia_lootbox</code> with tab-completion.<br>
<strong>Opening History</strong> — Per-player tracking with admin commands to view and clear.<br>
<strong>Anti-Autoclicker</strong> — Rate-limiting protection against rapid-fire abuse.<br>
<strong>Command & XP Rewards</strong> — Execute server commands and grant XP as lootbox rewards.<br>
<strong>30+ Config Parameters</strong> — Cooldowns, broadcasts, hub, performance, security, animation, sounds, free lootbox timers.<br>
<strong>Bilingual</strong> — Full English and French localization.
</p>

<p align="center"><strong>Arcadia LootBox</strong> est un systeme de lootbox premium concu pour le serveur <strong>Arcadia: Echoes of Power</strong>.<br>Propulse par <strong>Arcadia Lib</strong>, il offre deux types de lootbox, 50 cles integrees, des lootbox gratuites avec timer, l'integration au Hub Arcadia, et une configuration complete.</p>

<h3 align="center">Fonctionnalites Cles</h3>

<p align="center">
<strong>Deux types de Lootbox</strong> — "Weighted" (% par item) et "Guaranteed" (1 aleatoire + drop garanti).<br>
<strong>50 Items Cles</strong> — Donjon, Boutique, Vote, Trouvable, Evenement, Boss sur 10 tiers avec textures.<br>
<strong>Lootbox gratuites avec timer</strong> — Cooldown configurable (72h par defaut), reductible a 48h avec permissions.<br>
<strong>Integration Hub Arcadia</strong> — Navigateur client steampunk avec lien boutique configurable.<br>
<strong>6 Niveaux de Rarete</strong> — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic avec affichage colore.<br>
<strong>Broadcasts Serveur</strong> — Annonces serveur pour les drops rares.<br>
<strong>LuckPerms souple</strong> — Integration LuckPerms complete, fonctionne aussi sans LP.<br>
<strong>18 Commandes Admin</strong> — Gestion complete sous <code>/arcadia_lootbox</code> avec auto-completion.<br>
<strong>Historique d'Ouvertures</strong> — Suivi par joueur avec commandes admin.<br>
<strong>Anti-Autoclicker</strong> — Protection contre l'abus.<br>
<strong>Recompenses Commandes & XP</strong> — Commandes serveur et XP en recompenses.<br>
<strong>30+ Parametres</strong> — Configuration TOML complete.<br>
<strong>Bilingue</strong> — Localisation complete EN/FR.
